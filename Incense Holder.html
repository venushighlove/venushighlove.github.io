DIY INCENSE HOLDER

Created by fallfordiy.com

Materials

Air Clay, Clay Roller, Skewer, Paint, Gloss, Sand Paper

Instructions

Step 1

Roll out you clay and cut into a rectangle with the craft knife, smoothing the edges and top surface. Here you can make a stamp out of a small strip of metal. Press the stamp into the clay to create a pattern.

Step 2

Use the skewer to poke a hole through the other end of the rectangle.

Mask of the ends of the holders and paint. Then varnish the holders. Leave next to the incense sticks as a gentle hint to use instead of balancing them around and making a mess!

Step 3

Using a little more sheet metal make a wedge by folding a large triangle. Alternatively you could use an actual wedge. Push it under the hole end of the rectangle to create a slope. Leave the clay for 24 hours to harden.

Step 4

Mask of the ends of the holders and paint. Then varnish the holders. Leave next to the incense sticks as a gentle hint to use instead of balancing them around and making a mess!
