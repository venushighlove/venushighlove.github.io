WHO HVL IS

I, Bryana, am HVL.
In the midst of the worldwide pandemic, I created HVL. HVL stands for High Venus Love.
The reason why that is the name of this site is only becuase I had a vision that that was the name of my brand. My brand being a maker and collector or ceramic figures, dishes, jewlery trays, etc.
The reason why this "brand" or "blog" came about was because of that vision and due to my desire to create and share.
During the pandemic, it became a stressful and overwhelming time for me.
Creating this was an escape.
HVL was the escape from dealing with the constraintes of online schooling and a lockdown.
Along the way, there were moments of brainstoming and creating. This was a way of showing expression.
I want this to be a website to just admire and inspire.
Art is a free form of expression.
Anything created with emotion and thought is art.

WHAT HVL DOES

HVL will have items that have been created by me and other inspirations.
There will be updates on tutorials, weekly creations, and links to videos or images for inspirations.
This is a free platform to just explore expressionism. It is a simplistic website that has everything that has inspired me during this pandemic.
